About Us

The magazine was started by Sasha A. Braus in 2008. Braus hired Heidi Lynn to be its first editor. The first issue was published in October 2008 and soon was selling 100,000 copies.

Its editorial focus is on the daily needs and concerns of mothers with young children.

The glossy monthly features information about child health, safety, behavior, discipline and education.

There are also stories on women's health, nutrition, pregnancy, and beauty.

It is aimed primarily at parents with young children.
